Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any of our courses by visiting our campus or applying online through our website. Navigate to the Courses page, select your desired course, and click on the "Enroll Now" button to start the application process.

Are there any prerequisites for your courses?

Prerequisites vary by course. Beginner courses typically have no prerequisites, while intermediate and advanced courses may require prior knowledge or experience. Each course page lists specific prerequisites.

Do you offer financial assistance or payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans and scholarships for eligible students. Contact our financial aid office for more information about available options.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for most courses. If you're not satisfied with the course within the first week, you can request a full refund. After this period, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis.
