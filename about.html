Our Story

The Suntech Computer Education run by Sri Narayanaswamy Educational and Charitable Trust(9/2013). The Suntech Institute is providing quality computer education to all sections of society. Started with a view to catering to the educational needs of the socially, economically and educationally disadvantaged sections of society, the institute has been providing IT education to all, since 2009 in the Thirumanur, Ariyalur(Dt) region of the Cauvery delta.

The institute properly registered MSME under Central Govt of India. The Institute has conducted various Certificate courses and Diploma and one year courses. All courses affiliated to Bharat Sevak Samaj NDA Sponsored by Govt of India. The Institute takes interest in developing various skills among the students by offering enrichment, add-on and skill development courses. In order to develop computer skills among the students the Computer Literacy Programme (CLP) is offered to all the students. The curriculum and syllabi for courses include Environmental Studies, Value Education and Gender Studies to make education a holistic one to the young students. Various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities are conducted to supplement the curricular aspects of the Programmes.

Our Mission

The Institute strives hard to realize its vision by working towards achieving the following mission statements:

The institution aims to provide quality computer and IT education to all, with a special focus on underprivileged sections of society. It empowers students with up-to-date knowledge, practical skills, and ethical values to meet national and global demands. By nurturing talent, promoting academic excellence, and encouraging research and innovation, it strives to enhance employability and leadership qualities. The goal is to bridge the gap between theory and practice, fostering a dynamic learning environment that inspires continuous growth, creativity, and professional excellence in the field of Computer Science and beyond.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in computer and IT education that transforms individuals into skilled, ethical, and innovative professionals. We envision an inclusive learning ecosystem that empowers students from all backgrounds to become global leaders, pioneers in research, and lifelong learners who drive technological advancement and societal progress.
